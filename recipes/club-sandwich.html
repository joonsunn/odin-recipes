Club Sandwich

Description

Club Sandwich is basically chicken sandwich.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ham
  • Mustard sauce
  • Processed Cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Slice crusts off bread.
  2. Butter the bread.
  3. Assemble layer by layer.
  4. Enjoy!
