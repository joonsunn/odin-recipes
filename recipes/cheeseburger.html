Cheeseburger

Description

Cheeseburger is my favourite food. I crave for it if I've not eaten it for a long time. It is my go-to comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Sesame bun
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mustard sauce
  • Processed Cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cut the sesame buns in half, butter both cut sides, and toast them on the pan. Remove when done.
  2. Shape ground beef into gold ball sized spheres, flatten it into a patty, and grill on pan.
  3. Crack some pepper over it, and apply a bit of salt. Flip after 3 minutes.
  4. Put cheese on it. Remove when cheese is melted.
  5. Assemble the burger by applying a bit of mustard on the bottom bun, putting the patty on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce on top of it, then tomatoes.
  6. Finally put the top bun over it and enjoy!
