Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a simple chinese dish to use up leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Rice (preferably overnight)
  • Egg
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Charsiu
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up pan/wok.
  2. Put oil. Once oil is shimmering, put in egg and stir vigorously.
  3. Put rice in, smash the rice to break it up and also to break up the egg into small pieces.
  4. Put frozen veggies.
  5. Season to taste.
  6. Enjoy!
